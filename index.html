About Us

Welcome to Pop's Pizza & Pasta, your favorite Italian restaurant in Coquitlam, BC! We're a family-owned and operated business that has been serving our community for over two decades.

Our story began when our founder, Pop, immigrated to Coquitlam from Italy in the early 1990s. He brought with him his passion for cooking and his family's traditional recipes, and soon opened a small pizzeria in the heart of the city.

Over the years, Pop's Pizza & Pasta has grown and evolved, but our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer service has remained unchanged.
